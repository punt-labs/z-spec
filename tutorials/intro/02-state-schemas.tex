\section{Constants}

\begin{axdef}
  maxMsgs : \nat
\where
  maxMsgs = 100
\end{axdef}

\section{State}

\begin{schema}{Inbox}
  msgs : \power MSG \\
  flag : MSG \pfun Flag \\
  from : MSG \pfun ADDR
\where
  \dom flag = msgs \\
  \dom from = msgs \\
  \# msgs \leq maxMsgs
\end{schema}

Every message in the inbox has a flag and a sender.
The invariants ensure that \texttt{flag} and \texttt{from}
are defined for exactly the messages that exist.
