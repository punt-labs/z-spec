\section{Given Sets}

\begin{zed}
  [MSG, ADDR]
\end{zed}

\texttt{MSG} represents email messages and \texttt{ADDR}
represents email addresses. We declare that these kinds of
things exist without defining their internal structure.

\section{Free Types}

\begin{zed}
  Flag ::= flSeen | flUnseen | flFlagged
\end{zed}

\begin{zed}
  Folder ::= fdInbox | fdSent | fdTrash | fdArchive
\end{zed}

Free types enumerate every possible value. The prefixes
(\texttt{fl}, \texttt{fd}) prevent name collisions when
loaded into a model checker.
